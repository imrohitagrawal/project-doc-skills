# Status — current stack and phase

Last reviewed: 2019-03-01

This fixture carries a last-reviewed stamp that is years old. The staleness check
is WARN-only, so the page does not fail the build, but the verifier must raise a
warning that the stamp is older than the threshold and the facts need a re-check.

The stack and phase named on a status page go out of date faster than anything
else, which is exactly why the stamp is checked. A stamp this old means nothing on
the page can be trusted without a fresh review.
